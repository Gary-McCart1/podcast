About Our Host - Arthur Lozinski

Arthur Lozinski is a seasoned entrepreneur with a deep-rooted passion for enterprise software, technology, and the intricate mechanics of business. As the co-founder of Oomnitza, he has transformed his expertise in complex business processes into groundbreaking solutions that empower organizations worldwide. Now, he brings that same curiosity and drive to Founders in Focus, sitting down with the world’s most inspiring entrepreneurs, executives, and change-makers. Through candid, unfiltered conversations, Arthur uncovers the lessons, challenges, and innovations that define success—offering listeners an insider’s perspective on what it truly takes to build and lead in today’s world.
